Rules and Regulations

FASHION SHOW

  • One entry per college.
  • Theme selection is open to the team.
  • Participants should carry their own props.
  • Should carry their tracks in pen drive only.
  • Negative marking is there if participants exceed time limit.
  • A green room would be provided for the changing purpose.
  • Time limit for every team would be 10 minutes. (including set up and performance both).
  • Team will be judged on the costumes, theme walking stance and attitude.
  • Decision of the judges will be final and binding.

STAND-UP COMEDY

  • Individual Participants should be there.
  • Time limit is maximum 4-5 minutes. There will be a buzzer after 3 minutes 15 seconds.
  • Negative marking for exceeding the time limit.
  • Inappropriate comments should not permit. (e.g. sex, religion, racism etc.)
  • Participants will be judged on the basis of contents, fluency, spontaneity, presentation and sense of humour.
  • Highest weightage would be placed on originality and delivery of content.
  • Decision of the judges will be final.

CRAZY LEGS

  • Participants have to carry their own props.
  • Maximum 8 members will be there in team(group dance).
  • Minimum 3 and maximum 6 minutes for group dance and solo dance.
  • Music track should be carried by their own with a backup to avoid last minute chaos and should be provided prior to the event in the pen drive.
  • Vulgarity in dance moves, costumes and songs will not be permitted.
  • Decision of the judges will be final.

MUSIC MARATHON

  • Participants have to carry their own props. (Guitar, Piano and etc.)
  • Participants should come with 4-5 songs.
  • Participants can participate either in Solo or Group.
  • Group members can have maximum of 6 members in one group.
  • Time duration for each performance is maximum 5 minutes, exceeding the limit lead to negative marking.
  • Instruments are allowed for both Group and Solo performance.
  • If there is Karaoke then participants must bring MP3 format file in pen drive.
  • Vulgarity in songs are not allowed.
  • Decision of the judges will be final.

BEAT BOXING / RAPPING

  • Be Respectful
  • Be quite during opponent’s round.
  • No physical contact should be there.
  • No copy should be allowed.
  • Participants will be judged on the basis of performance, presence, content, language and originality.
  • All the rappers have an opinion of using a backing track for their performance. (but point preference will be given on performance only)
  • No obscenity of any kind either clothes or languages.
  • No hate speeches should be there.
  • Hindi and English languages are only allowed.
  • Decision of the judges will be final.

PICTURESQUE

  • Theme will be given on spot.
  • Photography should be relevant to theme.
  • Maximum 3 to 4 photographs from each should be given.
  • Photographs should be clicked within the ISME campus only.
  • 2 hours will be given for the whole photography.
  • Students have to submit soft copies of their photographs by pen drive only.
  • Winner will be judged on the basis of best creativity, originality, uniqueness and attractiveness.
  • Decision of the judges will be final.
